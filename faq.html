Frequently Asked Questions

Understand why Crast exists.

Crast is a Windows desktop tool that automates desktop activity using configurable presets and manual controls. It runs locally on your computer and puts you in control of how repetitive desktop interactions get handled.

Crast is for freelancers, remote workers, testers, developers, presenters, basically anyone who wants to automate routine desktop activity on their own Windows computer.

But at its core, it's built for people who get things done, work with integrity, and deliver quality work, but still feel stressed out and held back by modern micromanagement systems.

Not all computer work involves constant typing or mouse movement.

A lot of people spend time reading documentation, sitting in meetings, planning projects, sketching ideas, or just thinking through a hard problem. These things can involve very little physical input.

For some remote workers, activity-based monitoring creates unnecessary pressure during these moments, even when real work is getting done.

Crast gives you more control over repetitive desktop activity while you focus on the actual work.

We also believe working from home should feel like working from home. That means having the flexibility to think, learn, take a short break, or handle everyday moments without every single second of physical activity defining the value of your work.

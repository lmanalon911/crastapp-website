How It Works

Everything you need to know about how Crast works.

Crast runs quietly in the background and mimics normal computer activity: mouse movement, scrolling, keyboard taps, and browser tab switching. That keeps your measured activity level steady instead of just reflecting whatever you're literally doing second to second.

Low, Mid, and High are one-tap presets targeting roughly 45%, 65%, and 85% overall activity respectively, each varying naturally within its band. Manual mode opens a control panel where you set exact Mouse %, Keyboard %, and Overall % yourself, plus how much random variation to allow.

Crast runs hidden with no visible window during normal use. It's controlled entirely through global hotkeys and a small system tray icon that always shows the current status. The Manual mode control panel only appears when you explicitly open it.

Windows 10 or 11. Everything Crast needs is already built into Windows, so there's nothing extra to install. And no, you don't need admin rights.

Crast uses a license key. Each key works on a set number of devices depending on the tier you buy (Single, Dual, or Triple), and we check it against our activation server. Check the Download page for tier details.

Just hit the in-app "Check for Update" button. It grabs the latest release and installs it automatically, no reinstall needed.

Your license works on however many devices your tier includes. Need to free up a slot, or have questions about your activation? Just reach out through the in-app Report Issue button or email support.
